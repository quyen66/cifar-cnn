================================================================================
TRADE-OFF EXPLANATION
================================================================================

Cannot optimize all 3 metrics simultaneously:

• Low FNR (aggressive) ←→ High FPR (many false alarms)
  Example: Tight thresholds catch more attacks but flag benign clients

• High DR (aggressive) ←→ High FPR (many false alarms)
  Example: Config optimized for gradient attacks sacrifices precision

• Low FPR (conservative) ←→ High FNR (miss attacks)
  Example: Loose thresholds avoid false alarms but miss sophisticated attacks

Choose config based on which metric is MOST CRITICAL for your deployment!
